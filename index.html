I photograph the city at the moments it forgets it's being watched.

Anil Kumar is a Toronto-based photographer working across street, portrait and editorial work. What started as a way to decompress after long days in tech became a second, quieter discipline — one frame at a time, mostly on foot, mostly before the city wakes up.

This site is also a live working archive: every shoot gets added here directly, unedited copy and all, so the index always reflects what's actually being made right now.
